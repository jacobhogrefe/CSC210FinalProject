Multiplexer Choice Indications
------------------------------
Program Counter:
00 - Jump or Halt operation
01 - Standard Operation 
10 - Skips next instruction based on condition

Accumulator: 
00 - Input data
01 - Ram output
10 - ALU output

Op Code 8 Bit Output:
000 - 01011000 - 58
001 - 01011010 - 5a
010 - 01010001 - 51
011 - 01011100 - 5c
100 - 11011100 - DC
101 - 01000000 - 40
110 - 
111 - 00000000 - 0
